What to expect when you need a website

Scott Frees, Frees Consulting and Development LLC

Learn what small businesses need to know about getting their first website up and how to make it work harder for them!

SCBP member, Scott Frees, will walk you through the myriad of web presence options you have as a small business owner. The talk will overview the types of sites businesses tend to need – from a simple “here’s my company” site, to ecommerce, all the way up to customer portals and custom sites.

You’ll learn what’s hard (expensive), and what isn’t (inexpensive and DIY) – so you can make informed decisions when talking with vendors. You’ll also learn how different “buzzwords” like SEO, UX, responsive design, and analytics actually help you and your business – and how to cut through a lot of the tech speak along the way.

Finally, you’ll learn how to sleep at night - by understanding what types of security your site should have, and just how easy most of it is to setup.
